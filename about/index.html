Hello there!

Both Thanasis and Dimitri are seasoned developers, each a domain expert at their respective field. They have both founded their own starts and know what it means to be in the startup grind looking for answers and advice.

We started the Listen Ship Repeat podcast because we saw a lack of understanding and appreciation for the challenged posed on the actual "building" part of a Startup. Having 20+ years of experience, each, working with startups, we are starting to see patterns and specific paths that can be followed on each occasion, stage and situation a startup finds itself.

In each episode we select a big challenge every startup will or has faced at some point and dive deep into it, providing simple digestible advice on how to navigate through the treacherous waters.
